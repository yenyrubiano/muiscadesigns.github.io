My Skills

I enjoy creating people-centric, delightful experiences.

I use different facets and tools of design to create and execute solutions for user experience deficiencies. My process includes analyzes, designs, testing, launching, and evaluation. I do this by brainstorming solutions to current pain points, taking input from stakeholders, acting as a liaison between teams, and helping compose mock-ups through wireframes and interactive prototypes.

I drive brand awareness and lead generation through digital channels; such as social media, website, search engine rankings, email, display advertising, and blog. I like to focus on key performance indicators (KPIs) to properly measure performance B2B and B2C.

As a UI designer, my goal is to make SaaS, websites, and apps visually appealing and easy to navigate. As a UX designer, I focus on the experience a user has with the interface. My goal is to always design products that are functional, accessible, and enjoyable to use.

I have HTML/CSS skills under my sleeve. I have the ability to transform my designs into interactive interfaces. I have an eye for detail and a rock-solid understanding of what is needed behind the scenes for a site or application to run.

During the course of my career, I have had the opportunity to manage my own business. I have the ability to outline projects, assign tasks, foresee obstacles, find creative solutions, meet deadlines, and show stakeholders a plan to succeed are crucial to excellent management.
